What we do

  • Host sessions on programming catered to beginners
  • Promote Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) in campus
  • Encourage members to become Linux Superusers

Liberate The Mind

Subscription services, paywalls, and licenses. Everyday the world is heading towards a place where one is lead to believe that good things come from paid software. We like to believe otherwise. Great things have been made with OSS, in fact most of the web itself runs on Linux, a FOSS kernel. Liberating oneself from the very notion of paid software superiority is encapsulated in our motto, Liberate The Mind. In the words of Linus Torvalds, software is like sex; It's good only when it's free.
